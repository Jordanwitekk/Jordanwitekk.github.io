Greetings from London

Animated Postcard Project

Greetings from London is a postcard I designed for one of my animation classes at UW-Milwaukee. The animation was to incorporate icons that set London apart from other cities, and ones that when someone saw them, they would immediately recognize that the city was London.I chose icons that were very familiar to others such as the red double decker bus, the Eye ferris wheel, and telephone booth.

MOODZILLA

DVC Healthy Cities Project

Moodzilla is a game designed for 6 - 12 year olds who struggle with mental health. Mental Health is as important for a child as their physical health. They can disrupt a child’s focus and functioning at home, school and in their community. If left untreated these mental health issues can manifest anger issues and behavioral issues at school and home, feelings of low self-worth and even suicide. Teaching children at a young age about mental health, that it is ok to not be ok and to not be ashamed to ask for help are all things that will make a child’s life brighter for themselves and those around them.

The Mitchell Park Domes

Typography Poster Project 3

We were to research and create a poster of a location or item in Milwaukee of our choice, using a typeface that helps convey the feeling of that location. Working on developing a number of poster design concepts, headings and subheadings to choose from. Using Adobe Illustrator and Indesign to create the design that is clear and concise and entices the viewer to read the poster and learn about the Milwaukee landmark chosen. Working on developing a number of ideas to show a step by step process, a variety of ideas, designs and concepts. Keeping detailed records of the process, steps, and final results of the Milwaukee poster project.
